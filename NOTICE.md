# Notice

This repository contains an independently written, transformative scientific-writing workflow.

The design was informed in part by the pedagogical framework in:

Hilary Glasman-Deal, *Science Research Writing for Non-Native Speakers of English*, Imperial College Press, 2010.

The original book, its PDF, chapter text, exercises, answer keys, and extended examples are not included. The materials here paraphrase general writing principles, reorganize them into a Codex workflow, and add original routing, integrity checks, Chinese-to-English translation guidance, manuscript-audit logic, target-corpus adaptation, and evaluation cases.
